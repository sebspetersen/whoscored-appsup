Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2025

Whoscored ("the App") is published by Knolden Ventures ("we", "our", "us"). Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Do Not Collect

  • No personal information such as name, address, or phone number.
  • No location data.
  • No contacts or photos.

2. Data We Collect

The App only collects anonymous, aggregated usage statistics (e.g., screen views, crash reports) through Apple‑provided services to help us improve stability and performance.

3. How We Use Data

Aggregated analytics help us understand which features are most popular and where improvements are needed. We do not use collected data for advertising or share it with third‑parties.

4. Support Communications

If you email knoldenventures@gmail.com, the message will include your email address and any information you voluntarily provide. We use this solely to address your enquiry and improve customer support.

5. Data Retention

Usage statistics are retained in aggregate form for up to 24 months. Support emails are retained for as long as necessary to resolve your request.

6. Your Rights

You may request deletion of any support emails you have sent by contacting us at the address above.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Significant changes will be noted on this page and, where appropriate, in‑app.

For questions about privacy, please email knoldenventures@gmail.com.
